Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Question: How do I register for courses?

    • Answer: To register for courses, login to your student portal and navigate to the registration page. Follow the on-screen instructions to select your desired courses and complete the registration process.

  • Question: How can I reset my password?

    • Answer: If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email or SMS.
